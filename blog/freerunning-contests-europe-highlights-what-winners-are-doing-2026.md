## The pressure factor in Europe circuits

High crowd energy and stacked fields can distort decision-making.

Top athletes usually protect three mental rules:

- Never improvise your first risk-heavy section
- Keep one reliable transition after each major difficulty segment
- Decide backup-line triggers before event day

This mindset work is often the hidden separator between semifinal exits and finals consistency.

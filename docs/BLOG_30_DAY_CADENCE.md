# JumpFlix 30-Day Blog Cadence (Video-Intent SEO)

Goal: increase organic watch-intent traffic and convert searchers into video sessions on JumpFlix.

This plan assumes your current 10 articles are already created and focuses on:

- smart publish order,
- internal link compounding,
- CTR tuning,
- quick iteration based on query behavior.

## Core KPIs to track weekly

- Search impressions (by target cluster)
- Search CTR (page + query level)
- Avg engaged time per article
- Click-through rate from article to JumpFlix watch links (`/?q=`)
- Indexed pages count for `/blog/*`

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

### Monday: Publish or major refresh

- One primary page publish or substantial update.

### Tuesday: Internal linking and related-post tuning

- Add/update links from 2-3 existing articles to newest page.

### Wednesday: SERP/AI readability pass

- Tighten intro direct answer + one list/table section.

### Thursday: CTA and watch-path optimization

- Improve 1-2 article CTA sections and deep links.

### Friday: Metrics review + backlog decisions

- Promote winners, fix losers, set next week priorities.

## Fast Rules for Month 1 Decisions

If an article has:

- High impressions + low CTR: change title/description first.
- Good CTR + low watch click-through: improve CTA placement and deep links.
- Good watch click-through + low engagement: strengthen structure and add useful subsections.
- Zero traction after 2 weeks: expand query coverage with new FAQ and move-specific examples.
